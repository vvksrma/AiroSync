🛡️ Health Impact & Recommendations

💨 PM2.5 (Fine Particles)

  • •Ultra-fine particles penetrate deep into lungs
  • •Can cross into the bloodstream
  • •Linked to cardiovascular disease
  • •Affects neurological development

🌬️ PM10 (Coarse Particles)

  • •Irritates upper respiratory airways
  • •Causes coughing and wheezing
  • •Aggravates pre-existing asthma
  • •Reduces lung function over time

✅ Safety Measures

  • •Wear N95 mask when AQI > 100
  • •Close windows on high pollution days
  • •Run HEPA air purifiers indoors
  • •Limit strenuous outdoor exercise
